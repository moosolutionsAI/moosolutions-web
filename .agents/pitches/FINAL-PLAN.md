# MooSolutions.ai — Unified Creative Direction

## Synthesized from 4 Director Pitches

### Concept: "Obsidian Signal" meets "The Quiet Revolution"

MooSolutions.ai is a signal in the noise — a studio that builds tools so good they disappear into your workflow. The site is a progressive argument: establish the mind behind the brand, name the problem, prove it with products, invite action.

## Brand Identity

### Color Palette (Art Director — unanimous dark premium)
- `#09090B` — Void Black (primary background)
- `#141417` — Surface Dark (cards, section backgrounds)
- `#1E1E24` — Elevated Surface (hover states, overlays)
- `#7C3AED` — Deep Violet (primary accent)
- `#A78BFA` — Soft Violet (glows, highlights)
- `#F0F0FF` — Luminance White (hero headlines)
- `#E4E4F0` — Ghost White (body text)
- `#6B6B7A` — Muted Steel (secondary text)

### Typography
- **Display**: Cabinet Grotesk or Neue Montreal (heavy, wide-tracked)
- **Technical/Labels**: Geist Mono (letter-spacing: 0.2em, violet)
- **Body**: Inter Variable (invisible, always legible)
- **Scale**: Aggressive — hero 96-120px, body 16-18px

### Voice (Brand Storyteller — winner)
- Quietly confident, precise, warmly intelligent, understated wonder
- **Tagline**: "Less interface. More you."
- **Manifesto line**: "We build tools that disappear."

## Animation Language (Motion Director — "Liquid Gravity")

### Spring System
- **Primary**: stiffness 100, damping 15, mass 1 (~600ms, confident arrivals)
- **Soft**: stiffness 50, damping 20, mass 1.5 (~900ms, parallax/background)
- **Snap**: stiffness 400, damping 25, mass 0.5 (~200ms, micro-interactions)
- **GSAP fallback**: power3.out (scroll-pinned), power2.inOut (cross-fades)

### Key Animations
1. **Hero**: Word-by-word stagger (0.08s), logo SVG draw-on, cursor-reactive particle field
2. **Hero→Services transition**: Hero compresses/sinks, services rise over it (z-depth illusion)
3. **Services**: Cards expand from horizontal lines, diagonal stagger
4. **Portfolio**: Horizontal scroll carousel pinned to viewport, Ken Burns settle
5. **Contact**: Iris reveal (clipPath circle expansion), field border draws

### Lenis Config
- Duration: 1.2, wheelMultiplier: 0.8, touchMultiplier: 1.5
